* Print star in following format

```txt
Input: 5
Output:
*        *
**      **
***    ***
****  ****
**********
****  ****
***    ***
**      **
*        *
```

{% endtab %}{% tab title='BJ_2445.py' %}

```py
a = int(input())
for i in range(1, a + 1):
  print('*' * i + ' ' * (2 * (a - i)) + '*' * (i))
for k in range(1, a + 1):
  print('*' * (a - k) + ' ' * (2 * k) + '*' * (a - k))
```
