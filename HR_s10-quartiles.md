* Print quartile Q1, Q2, Q3

```txt
Input:
9
3 7 8 5 12 14 21 13 18

Output:
6
12
16
```

{% endtab %}{% tab title='HR_s10-quartiles.py' %}

```py
from statistics import median
input()
arr= list(sorted(map(int, input().split())))
t = int(len(arr) / 2)
if len(arr) % 2==0:
  L = arr[:t]
  U = arr[t:]
else:
  L = arr[:t]
  U = arr[t+1:]

print(int(median(L)))
print(int(median(arr)))
print(int(median(U)))
```
