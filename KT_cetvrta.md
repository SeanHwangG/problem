* Given coordinate of three corners of rectangle, find the last corner

```txt
Input:
1 3
2 3
2 5

Output: 1 5
```

{% endtab %}{% tab title='KT_cetvrta.py' %}

```py
x1, y1 = map(int, input().split())
x2, y2 = map(int, input().split())
x3, y3 = map(int, input().split())
print(x1 ^ x2 ^ x3, y1 ^ y2 ^ y3)
```
