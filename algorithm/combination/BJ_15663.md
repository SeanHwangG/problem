* Sequence of choosing M from N natural numbers

```txt
Input:
4 2
9 7 9 1

Output:
1 7
1 9
7 1
7 9
9 1
9 7
9 9
```

{% endtab %}{% tab title='BJ_15663.py' %}

```py
N, M = map(int, input().split())
li = list(sorted(map(int, input().split())))
def backtrack(cur, N, M):
  if len(cur) == M:
    print(*cur)
    return
  for i in range(N):
    if (not cur or cur[-1] != li[i]) and li[i] != li[i - 1]:
      cur.append(li[i])
      backtrack(cur, N, M)
      cur.pop()
backtrack([], N, M)
```
