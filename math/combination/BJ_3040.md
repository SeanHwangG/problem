# [BJ_3040](https://acmicpc.net/problem/3040)

* en

  ```en
  Out of 9 numbers, print 7 numbers that sums up to 100
  ```

* tc

  ```tc
  Input:
  7
  8
  10
  13
  15
  19
  20
  23
  25

  Output:
  7
  8
  10
  13
  19
  20
  23
  ```

## Solution

* py

  ```py
  from itertools import combinations
  for n in combinations([int(input()) for _ in [0] * 9], 7):
    if sum(n)==100:
      print(*n)
      break
  ```
