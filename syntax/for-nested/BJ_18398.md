# [BJ_18398](https://acmicpc.net/problem/18398)

Print sum and multiple for N testcase for M pairs

```txt
Input: 3  # N Testcase
2         # M Pairs
20 30
40 60
1         # M Pairs
1 1
3         # M Pairs
2 2
3 3
3 2

Output: 50 600  # 20 + 30, 20 * 30
100 2400  # 40 + 60, 40 * 60
2 1
4 4
6 9
5 6
```

## Solution

* py

  ```py
  for _ in range(int(input())):
    for _ in range(int(input())):
      a, b = map(int, input().split())
      print(a + b, a * b)
  ```
