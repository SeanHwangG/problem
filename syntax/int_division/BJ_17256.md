* tc

  ```tc
  Input: 15 16 17
  19 32 90
  Output: 2 2 75  # 19 = 17 + 2, 32 = 16 * 2, 90 = 15 + 75

  Input: 15 8 15
  22 8 22
  Output: 7 1 7   # 22 = 15 + 7, 8 = 8 * 1, 22 = 15 + 7
  ```

## Solution

* py

  ```py
  ax, ay, az = map(int, input().split())
  cx, cy, cz = map(int, input().split())
  print(cx - az, cy // ay, cz - ax)
  ```
